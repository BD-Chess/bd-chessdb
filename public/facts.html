15 Powerful Facts About the ChessDB Engine Database

ChessDB.cn is the living, breathing megabrain of online chess—powered by thousands of CPUs running Stockfish, the full 7-piece Syzygy DTZ50 tablebases, TCEC tournament feeds, and continuous user-contributed analysis.

Here's why ChessDB is even better than carrying a chess supercomputer in your pocket:

  1. Outperforms champions & supercomputers: Unmatched analysis depth and quality.
  2. Over 100,000 CPU-years of analysis: Centuries of computation instantly accessible.
  3. Saves 30–60 minutes per move: Depth-50 results delivered in milliseconds.
  4. 48.6+ billion unique positions: Massive and expanding database (~1 TB).
  5. Depth 50+ evaluations: Deep insights far beyond typical engine reach.
  6. 5–10 candidate moves per position: Multiple top-tier engine moves.
  7. Coverage of rare openings: Even unconventional lines fully analyzed.
  8. Daily database updates: Real-time improvements from user queries and TCEC tournament uploads.
  9. Integrated TCEC feeds: High-quality status games (Div P, L1, Superfinal, etc.) are ingested via pgn2cdb to upgrade eval lines.
  10. Free, instant browser access: No setup required.
  11. Integrated 7-piece Syzygy tables: Perfect endgames with centipawn accuracy (~18 TB).
  12. API & UCI integration: Seamless embedding in any chess tool.
  13. Live AI fallback: Automatic Stockfish suggestions if needed.
  14. Backward propagation: Enhanced accuracy from deep sub-analyses.
  15. Pure engine opening book: No human bias, just rigorous analysis.

Detailed Explanations

Champions vs. ChessDB

Traditionally, grandmasters and top engines guided chess improvement. ChessDB eclipses both with vast computational power, delivering move quality unmatched by human or typical engine analysis.

Precomputed CPU-Years Explained

A single position at depth-50 takes many minutes on a strong PC. ChessDB has precomputed billions of positions, equivalent to hundreds of millennia!

Instant Move Evaluations

No waiting for engines—ChessDB serves deep results in milliseconds, letting you focus on strategy.

TCEC Tournament Feeds

ChessDB regularly ingests full PGN dumps from Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC)—including high-quality status games like Division P, League 1, Superfinal, and more—via pgn2cdb, upgrading or replacing entries with deeper, more accurate analysis.

Backward Propagation Clarity

Insights from deeper child positions flow upward, improving evaluation accuracy and stability.

Live AI Fallback Feature

If a novel position isn't yet in the database, ChessDB seamlessly falls back to Stockfish for real-time analysis.

API & UCI for Developers

Direct REST API endpoints and standard UCI commands make embedding ChessDB’s power into your tools effortless.

Unbiased Engine-Built Openings

Pure computational analysis, free from human bias, forms the ultimate opening guide.

Grob Test Suite and Curated Lines

Even the rarest openings are covered, with nearly a million lines hand-curated for completeness and accuracy.
