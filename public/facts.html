15 Powerful Facts About the ChessDB Engine Database

ChessDB.cn is the living, breathing megabrain of online chess—powered by thousands of CPUs running Stockfish and the full 7-piece Syzygy DTZ50 tablebases.

Here's why ChessDB is even better than carrying a chess supercomputer in your pocket:

  1. Outperforms champions & supercomputers: Unmatched analysis depth and quality.
  2. Over 100,000 CPU-years of analysis: Centuries of computation instantly accessible.
  3. Saves 30–60 minutes per move: Depth-50 results delivered in milliseconds.
  4. 48.6+ billion unique positions: Massive and expanding database (~1 TB).
  5. Depth 50+ evaluations: Deep insights far beyond typical engine reach.
  6. 5–10 candidate moves per position: Multiple top-tier engine moves.
  7. Coverage of rare openings: Even unconventional lines fully analyzed.
  8. Daily database updates: Real-time improvements from user queries.
  9. Free, instant browser access: No setup required.
  10. Global distributed computing: University and enthusiast contributions.
  11. Integrated 7-piece Syzygy tables: Perfect endgames with centipawn accuracy (approx. 18 TB compressed).
  12. API & UCI integration: Seamless embedding in any chess tool.
  13. Live AI fallback: Automatic Stockfish suggestions if needed.
  14. Backward propagation: Enhanced accuracy from deep sub-analyses.
  15. Pure engine opening book: No human bias, just rigorous analysis.

Detailed Explanations

Champions vs. ChessDB

Traditionally, grandmasters and top engines guided chess improvement. ChessDB eclipses both with vast computational power, delivering move quality unmatched by human or typical engine analysis.

Precomputed CPU-Years Explained

A single position at depth-50 takes minutes on a strong PC. ChessDB has precomputed billions of positions, equivalent to hundreds of millennia of nonstop analysis, instantly at your disposal.

Instant Move Evaluations

Waiting for engines is history. ChessDB instantly provides analysis that would normally take hours locally, letting you focus on strategy and play.

Backward Propagation Clarity

ChessDB enriches positions by analyzing deeper "child positions." These insights flow upward, improving the accuracy and stability of the evaluations you see.

Live AI Fallback Feature

If a rare or novel position isn't yet analyzed, ChessDB seamlessly utilizes Stockfish in real-time, ensuring you always get guidance.

API & UCI for Developers

Easy-to-use API endpoints and standard UCI commands integrate ChessDB’s power directly into your chess application or interface.

Unbiased Engine-Built Openings

No human errors or favoritism. Pure, extensive computational analysis creates the ultimate opening guide.

Grob Test Suite and Curated Lines

The Grob Test Suite exemplifies ChessDB’s commitment to analyzing even the rarest openings thoroughly, with nearly a million carefully curated lines, ensuring comprehensive coverage of all possible chess positions.
